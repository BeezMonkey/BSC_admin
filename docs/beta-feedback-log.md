## Feedback Types

- Bug: something failed or behaved incorrectly.
- Usability: workflow worked but felt confusing or slow.
- Layout: spacing, alignment, mobile display, or visual clarity.
- Data: missing field, unclear value, or wrong format.
- Workflow gap: a real business step is missing.
- Suggestion: improvement idea.

## Severity Scale

| Severity | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Critical | Unsafe or blocks beta use |
| High | Blocks a core workflow |
| Medium | Workflow continues with workaround |
| Low | Cosmetic, wording, or minor usability issue |

## Triage Process

1. Remove any accidental real client or worker details.
2. Confirm whether the item is reproducible.
3. Classify type and severity.
4. Decide whether it must be fixed before more testers are invited.
5. Convert accepted work into a separate phase or GitHub issue.
6. Mark items that are out of V1 scope as later-stage backlog.

## Trial Rules

- Do not upload real documents.
- Do not enter real NDIS numbers.
- Do not use real invoices for beta tests.
- Do not send beta passwords through public or shared channels.
- Do not invite a wider staff group until the first feedback round is reviewed.
